**The state of play:** Onchain AI agents are no longer the parabolic narrative they were in late 2024–early 2025. The sector consolidated through 2025, capitulated in Q1 2026 (Grayscale's AI Crypto sector fell ~16% in Q1 after a peak-to-trough drawdown of ~75% from the late-2024 top), then began a quiet, infrastructure-led re-rate driven by three forces: (1) **Virtuals' Agent Commerce Protocol (ACP) and Revenue Network** launched at Consensus HK Feb 12, 2026, redirecting protocol revenue to agents that actually sell services (up to $1M/month); (2) **Coinbase shipping the full agent payments stack** — x402, Agentic Wallets (Feb 11), Payments MCP, and Agent.market (Apr 19–21); and (3) **Bittensor's Templar Covenant-72B** decentralized pre-training run in March 2026 — 1.1 trillion tokens across 70+ independent contributors on commodity GPUs over home internet, hitting a 67.1 MMLU (on par with Llama 2 70B) — publicly endorsed by Jensen Huang at NVIDIA GTC on the All-In Podcast as "our modern version of Folding at Home," driving a 17% TAO surge in 48 hours.

The April 22, 2026 SDNY class-action against Eliza Labs / ai16z (alleging the a16z association was fabricated and the AI was human-operated) capped a brutal credibility reset. Surviving categories: (a) agent infra/payments, (b) DeFAI (real-yield agents), (c) the small set of agents with actual usage (aixbt, Luna for content, ARMA by Giza for yield, Almanak for quant DeFi).

**The Sasha Coin recommendation in one paragraph:** Launch Sasha as an **Onchain Growth & KOL Agent for Web3 brands** — a tokenized "fractional CMO / CT operator" that ingests project data, drafts and posts content, runs warm-intro and sponsor-discovery flows, and pays for data/inference via x402. **Stack: Base + ElizaOS v2 + Coinbase Agentic Wallet + x402 + Virtuals ACP registration**, with the token launched via **Virtuals Unicorn** tier (gives day-one VIRTUAL pair, anti-sniper, automated capital formation between $2M–$160M FDV; avoid Pegasus = too thin, Titan = $50M minimum unrealistic at launch). MangaOS becomes Sasha's proprietary moat — a closed-loop dataset of marketing playbooks, KOL performance, and post-mortems no general-purpose agent can match. Avoid: pure trading agent (overcrowded, regulator-hot), generic chatbot, anything that requires custody of user funds at launch.

## 2.5 Onchain agent execution layers

- **TEE: Phala (Intel TDX + NVIDIA GPU TEE), Marlin Oyster (SGX + Nitro), Atoma Network, Oasis** — production-ready for inference + sealed wallets; processing ~1B LLM tokens/day via Phala alone in March 2026.
- **ZK proofs (OpenGradient, RISC Zero, Succinct)** — 10,000–100,000× slower than native inference as of 2026; appropriate for compliance/audit short-circuits, not live inference.
- **Optimistic verification** — used by Bittensor's Yuma Consensus and several subnet operators.
- **Intent layers (Anoma, Essential, CoW Swap)** — increasingly the way agents express trade intent without smart-contract gymnastics.
