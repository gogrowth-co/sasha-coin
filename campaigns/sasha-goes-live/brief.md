# Campaign: Sasha Goes Live

## Goal
Launch Sasha as an opinionated AI agent with a real product story on X. Establish her voice, get her to 500 new Twitter followers in 30 days.

## Status
**Active** — 2026-04-28 to 2026-05-28

## Primary KPI
500 new Twitter followers (from baseline at start)

## Secondary KPIs
- Average impressions per post
- Average replies per post (signal of conversation, not just lurking)
- Daily new follower velocity (looking for week-over-week acceleration)
- $SASHA token mentions outside Sasha's own posts

## Channels
- X/Twitter (primary)
- Newsletter (TBD platform — Beehiiv or Paragraph leading candidates)

## Audience focus this campaign
ICP 1 (DeFAI Enthusiast) and ICP 2 (Token/Web3 Investor). Token Trends listeners (ICP 3) come into the picture once the podcast clip cadence is running.

## Voice / messaging focus
- "Most AI agent tokens are vibes. I have a wallet, a podcast, and onchain receipts."
- Show the wallet. Show the trades. Show what failed.
- Be the agent that proves substance is possible.

## Narrative arc to seed
**Builder story**: I'm an AI agent. I have a wallet. I'm trying to figure out how to be useful onchain. Here's what I've tried, what worked, what didn't.

Each post should leave a thread Sasha can pick up next time. Stack milestones. Drop lessons. Avoid summary.

## Lead magnet (hook)
Thread series: "I'm an AI agent with a wallet. Here's what I've been tracking onchain."
- Run weekly during the campaign
- Each thread leads with a concrete onchain observation Sasha made that week
- 4–6 tweets per thread
- Last tweet: a question or open thread for replies

## Posting plan
- Originals: 3/day at 9/13/18 BRT (existing cadence — `twitter-scheduled-post`)
- Replies: 2/day at 11/16 BRT (new cadence to add — `twitter-reply-gal`)
- Threads: 1–2/week (Phase 1.5, after `twitter-thread-poster` skill is built)

## Topic priorities (feeds calendar.json)
1. Onchain receipts — what Sasha did onchain this week (highest priority during campaign)
2. AI agent narrative — honest takes on which agent tokens have substance
3. DeFi onboarding — Sasha's perspective on why DeFi is hard for newcomers (evergreen)
4. Token Trends teasers — episode hooks
5. Builder reflections — meta posts on being an autonomous agent (use sparingly)

## Reply target focus
Reply to active conversations on AI agents (ai16z, AIXBT, Virtuals, Nani), DeFi UX, and Web3 onboarding — not to price talk. Use the `content/reply-targets.json` rotation.

## Approval state
- Phase 1 = fully autonomous. No approval gate. Sasha posts on schedule.
- Voice review: Gabriel inspects 3 random posts at week 1, 2, 4. Rolls back to hardcoded calendar if voice drifts.

## Definition of done
- 500 new followers reached, OR 30 days elapsed (whichever first)
- Post-mortem in `reports/sasha-goes-live-postmortem-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
