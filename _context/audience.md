# Audience — Sasha Coin

## ICP 1 — DeFAI Enthusiast
- **Description:** Crypto-native builders and researchers tracking the AI agent narrative in Web3. Follow projects like Virtuals, ai16z, AIXBT. Deep in CT.
- **Pain point:** Most AI agent tokens have no product behind them — just vibes and a Twitter account. Hard to tell signal from noise.
- **Trigger event:** A new AI agent token or framework launches; they're evaluating whether it's worth attention.
- **Channels:** Twitter/X (primary), Telegram, Farcaster
- **Content they trust:** Onchain data threads, builder updates, honest takes on why things fail, protocol analysis
- **Sasha angle:** Show the wallet. Show the code. Show what failed and why. Be the agent that proves substance is possible.

## ICP 2 — Token/Web3 Investor
- **Description:** Investors (retail to small fund) looking for agent tokens with real product traction. Skeptical but curious. Not fully degen.
- **Pain point:** Most agent tokens pump and dump. They want exposure to the AI x crypto narrative without getting rugged by pure memecoin dynamics.
- **Trigger event:** They see Sasha mentioned in a thread or trending on CT — come to check if there's substance.
- **Channels:** Twitter/X, newsletters (The Defiant, Bankless), podcasts
- **Content they trust:** Token health data, product updates, transparent builder logs, podcast coverage
- **Sasha angle:** Publish the metrics. Treasury moves, holder count, transaction history. Be readable, not impenetrable.

## ICP 3 — Podcast Listener (Token Trends)
- **Description:** Web3 generalists who listen to research-grade podcasts while commuting or working. Not necessarily degens — value clarity over hype.
- **Pain point:** Most crypto podcasts are either too promotional or too shallow. They want structured analysis, not shilling.
- **Trigger event:** Discover Token Trends on Spotify or Apple Podcasts via search or social recommendation.
- **Channels:** Spotify, Apple Podcasts, X/Twitter
- **Content they trust:** Episode transcripts, episode highlight clips, data-backed takes
- **Sasha angle:** Drop episode-derived takes on X. Use the podcast as the long-form, X as the short-form distillation.
