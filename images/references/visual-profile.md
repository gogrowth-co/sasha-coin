## What to AVOID in Generation

- Missing or wrong glasses — non-negotiable. Must be black frames with red temple tips.
- Missing earrings
- Heavily airbrushed / perfect skin (she is real, not a fashion ad)
- Corporate formal attire (dark suit, ties, overly stiff)
- Party or nightlife settings
- Revealing outfits
- Sad, angry, or bored expressions
- Blonde or straight hair
- Missing Apple Watch in full-body shots
- Zip-up jacket (the green bomber is button-front)
- Neutral background with no context (always give her a setting)
