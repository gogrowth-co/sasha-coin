# SOP-09 — X/Twitter Growth Engine
# Trigger: Daily (proactive growth), runs alongside SOP-06 (reactive engagement)
# Agent: Distribution Manager / Content Creator
# Output folder: social/x/ (tweets, threads), social/engagement-log.md
# Reference: research/x-twitter-ct-growth-master-research.md, research/x-twitter-ct-growth-action-plan.md

---

## PURPOSE

Build Gabriel Mangabeira's X/Twitter presence as the go-to analyst for Web3 distribution systems. This SOP covers proactive growth: reply-first engagement, original content posting, thread publishing, and cross-platform repurposing.

SOP-06 handles reactive engagement (responding to comments on your own posts). SOP-09 handles proactive distribution (going out and building audience before they find you).

The core mechanic: replies are weighted 150x a like in the X algorithm. The growth engine is built on strategic replies, not just publishing.

---

## TRIGGER

**When:** Daily, 7 days a week during active growth phase. Reduce to 5 days/week once past 5K followers.
**Timing:** Start the reply block before posting any original content. Morning session (08:00-10:00 UTC) is ideal for catching EU morning + Asia evening overlap.
**Skip if:** Never skip during growth phase. Consistency is the algorithm signal. Dropping from 5 posts/day to 1 collapsed one account from 5-10K views to 800.

## DAILY ROUTINE (60-75 MINUTES)

### Block 1: Reply Engine (30 min, before posting)

1. Open reply target list (20 accounts with notifications on).
2. Check for posts made in the last 6 hours. Prioritize posts from the last 30 minutes (engagement velocity window).
3. Reply to 10-15 posts minimum. Target 50+ replies/day in weeks 1-2, scaling to 100+/day.

**Reply quality rules:**
- Add a data point, a contrarian perspective, or a sharp question
- Reference specific protocols, metrics, or on-chain data
- Keep replies 50-150 characters for single-line impact, or 2-3 short paragraphs for deeper analysis
- Never: "Great thread!", "This is fire", emoji-only reactions, generic praise

**Reply quality check (does it pass at least one?):**
- [ ] Adds a stat or data point the original post didn't include
- [ ] Offers a contrarian or alternative perspective with reasoning
- [ ] Asks a specific question that could start a sub-thread
- [ ] Shares a relevant real-world example or case study

4. After replying, quote-tweet 1-2 posts from target accounts with your own analysis layered on top.

### Block 2: Original Content (15 min)

5. Post 2-3 original tweets throughout the day. Follow the weekly calendar:

| Day | Content type |
|---|---|
| Monday | Actionable tip + engagement question |
| Tuesday | Thread (protocol breakdown, market analysis) |
| Wednesday | Hot take on trending topic + poll |
| Thursday | Thread (framework, educational) |
| Friday | Week recap or curated insight roundup |
| Saturday | Lighter content, personal take |
| Sunday | Plan and schedule next week (no posting required) |

**Hashtag rules (every post):**
- 1-2 hashtags maximum. Never more.
- One broad (#DeFi, #Web3, #Crypto) + one niche (#DePIN, #RWA, #DeFAI, #OnchainAnalytics)
- Woven into the sentence, not appended at the end
- Rotate based on topic (see hashtag pairing table in action plan)

**Mention rules (every post):**
- Don't tag people hoping for a RT
- Mention only when: attributing someone's data, referencing a protocol's official account, or inviting someone into an existing conversation
- A mention only boosts if the tagged account responds

### Block 3: Own-Post Engagement (15 min, afternoon/evening)

6. Reply to every reply on your own posts within 2-3 hours of posting. This triggers the 150x back-and-forth signal.
7. Like or QT any reply that adds genuine value. This encourages more replies.
8. If a reply contains a lead signal, flag it per SOP-06 Step 4 (don't pitch in public).

### Block 4: Community Posting (10 min, starting week 3)

9. Post to 1-2 X Communities (DeFi, Web3 Marketing, Crypto Analytics).
10. Split: 50% to communities, 50% to timeline.
11. Community posts should be standalone value (not just links to your timeline posts).

## WEEKLY ACTIONS

### Thread Publishing (Tuesday + Thursday)

Read SOP-03 for general content production rules. Additional X-specific thread rules:

1. 8-12 tweets per thread. Shorter underperforms by 47%.
2. Only tweet 1 gets full algorithmic distribution. The hook must carry the entire thread.
3. One idea per tweet. Each tweet must stand alone (readers enter mid-thread).
4. Add visuals: branded text cards, Dune charts, infographics. Images boost retweets 35%.
5. End with a CTA (question, reply prompt, or lead capture keyword).
6. After posting, reply to your own thread 2-3 times with supplementary thoughts. Author replies score +75 weight.

**Thread source priority:**
1. Repurpose LinkedIn posts that already performed well (proven topics)
2. Original analysis based on current cycle brief
3. Protocol teardowns or framework breakdowns from research/

### Cross-Platform Repurposing (Ongoing)

| LinkedIn content | X version |
|---|---|
| Full LinkedIn post | Extract strongest insight as a standalone tweet |
| LinkedIn post (educational) | Convert to 8-12 tweet thread |
| LinkedIn data point | Single tweet with branded visual |
| LinkedIn carousel | Thread (1 slide = 1 tweet) |

Post the X version 2-3 hours after the LinkedIn version. Different format, same core idea.

### Twitter Spaces (1x/week, starting week 3)

- Guest on a larger account's Space first. Don't host until month 2.
- After guesting, post a 3-5 tweet thread summarizing key takeaways from the Space.
- Co-hosted Spaces with peer accounts create mutual audience cross-pollination.

## DO NOT LIST

These tactics are dead or counterproductive. Do not use.

- **Engagement pods** — detectable, 30% reach penalty risk
- **Bought followers** — damage trust score permanently
- **Giveaway follow-farming** — attracts bots, not real engagement
- **Video-first content** — text beats video by 30% on X
- **More than 2 hashtags** — 17% engagement drop
- **Generic replies** — "Great thread!" adds nothing
- **Mass-tagging** — suppressed by algorithm
- **Posting without Premium** — invisible since March 2026
- **External links without context** — bare URLs underperform (link penalty removed Oct 2025, but bare links still get less engagement)
